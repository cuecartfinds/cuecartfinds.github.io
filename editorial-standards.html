Editorial standards

How CueCart Finds decides what belongs.

A strong affiliate site needs trust before traffic. These standards keep CueCart Finds practical, selective, and brand-safe as the product catalog grows.

01

Usefulness first

Products should have a practical reason to exist: save time, organize space, simplify a routine, or solve a clear problem.

02

Price and value fit

Affordable finds are prioritized. Higher-priced products need a stronger reason, better merchant trust, and clearer benefits.

03

Visual clarity

A good CueCart product should be easy to understand in a short video, carousel, product card, or simple landing page.

04

No fake urgency

Avoid countdowns, exaggerated claims, fake scarcity, or pressure-based sales language.

05

Affiliate transparency

Affiliate relationships should be disclosed clearly, and links should use approved merchant or network URLs only.

06

Update when needed

Product pages should be revised or removed if the item becomes unavailable, poorly reviewed, overpriced, or off-brand.

Operator checklist

Before a product goes live.

Confirm the exact product, source, affiliate approval, commission potential, price, public claims, and product-page disclosure before changing status to live.
